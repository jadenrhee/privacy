FunderLens Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

FunderLens is built local-first. Your research, notes, and pipeline stay on

your device. The one network call it makes is a lookup to a public nonprofit

data API, described below.

What FunderLens collects

Nothing on our side. FunderLens has no server, no analytics, no telemetry, no

error reporting, and no account system. We never see your searches, your saved

funders, or your notes.

Where your data lives

  • Saved funders, pipeline stages, notes, and settings are stored in your

browser profile using Chrome extension storage and IndexedDB, on your device

only.

  • The optional export writes a file to your computer at your request.

What the extension connects to

  • ProPublica Nonprofit Explorer API (projects.propublica.org). When you

look up a foundation or funder, FunderLens queries ProPublica's public,

free nonprofit dataset with the organization name or EIN you searched. These

are public IRS Form 990 records. The query contains only your search terms —

no personal data, no API key, no account. Results are cached locally. Your

use of that API is governed by ProPublica's terms (projects.propublica.org).

  • The tab you act on, only when you click. With activeTab + scripting

and the context-menu action, FunderLens can read a funder name from the page

you are on when you invoke it, to start a lookup. It does not read pages in

the background.

  • ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), only for payments. Handles upgrades via

Stripe; FunderLens stores only a cached paid/unpaid flag locally and sends it

no research data. See extensionpay.com/privacy and stripe.com/privacy.

  • An optional uninstall survey page opens if you remove the extension. It

is a static page; the link names the product, never you.

What FunderLens never does

  • No selling, sharing, or transmitting of your research to us or third parties.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.
  • No remote code.

Permissions, in plain language

  • storage, alarms: keep your pipeline and remind you about follow-ups.
  • sidePanel: show research beside the page.
  • contextMenus, activeTab, scripting: right-click a funder name to look it

up, reading the current tab only at that moment.

  • notifications: deadline and follow-up reminders.
  • host access to projects.propublica.org: run the public nonprofit lookup.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version ships with the extension update and is

posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/funderlens/privacy.
