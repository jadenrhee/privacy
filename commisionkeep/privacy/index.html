CommisionKeep Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

CommisionKeep is built local-first. The short version: your bookings and

commission records are yours, and they never leave your computer.

What CommisionKeep collects

Nothing. CommisionKeep has no server, no analytics, no telemetry, no error

reporting, and no account system. We cannot see your bookings, your

commissions, or the pages you visit, because nothing is ever transmitted to us.

Where your data lives

  • Bookings, commission records, and history are stored in your browser

profile using Chrome extension storage and IndexedDB, on your device only.

Deleting a record deletes it; there is no copy anywhere else.

  • Settings are stored with chrome.storage.local, also on your device.
  • The optional JSON/CSV export writes a file to your computer at your request.
  • CommisionKeep works fully offline.

What the extension connects to

  • The tab you capture from, only when you click. When you press "Capture"

in the popup, CommisionKeep reads the visible booking details on the tab you

are currently on, at that moment, using activeTab + scripting. It does

not read pages in the background and has no host permissions.

  • ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), only for payments. If you open the

upgrade page or the extension checks license status, ExtensionPay (a payment

service built on Stripe) handles it. CommisionKeep sends it no booking data

and stores only a cached paid/unpaid flag locally. We never see or store your

card details, name, or email. See extensionpay.com/privacy and

stripe.com/privacy.

  • An optional uninstall survey page opens if you remove the extension. It

is a static page; the link names the product, never you.

Licence check

CommisionKeep bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, CommisionKeep asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with CommisionKeep stays

on your computer.

Permissions, in plain language

  • storage, alarms: keep your bookings and remind you about due/overdue

commissions.

  • activeTab, scripting: read the visible booking on the tab you are on,

only when you click Capture.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version ships with the extension update and is

posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/commisionkeep/privacy. The local-only

design is the product; we do not intend to change it.
