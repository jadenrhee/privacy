HaulGuard Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

HaulGuard is a local-first Chrome extension. The short version: your data stays

on your computer, and we could not read it even if we wanted to.

What HaulGuard stores

Carrier profiles, loads, accessorial line items, claim statuses, and settings

you enter are stored in your browser's local extension storage

(chrome.storage.local) on your device. Nothing is uploaded. There is no

account, no server, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

What HaulGuard reads

Nothing. HaulGuard has no host permissions and no content script on any site

you visit. It never asks for a load board, broker portal, or email login. All

load data is entered by you.

Network traffic

The only network communication in the extension is with ExtensionPay

(extensionpay.com), the payment provider for Pro subscriptions. When you open

the payment page or the extension checks subscription status, ExtensionPay

processes that request under its own privacy policy

(https://extensionpay.com/privacy). Card details are handled by Stripe and

never touch HaulGuard. HaulGuard stores only a paid/unpaid flag locally.

If you never upgrade, the extension still makes this status check; it carries

no personal data beyond what ExtensionPay needs to answer "is this browser's

install paid".

Emails and invoices

HaulGuard generates claim emails and invoices as text on your screen. It never

sends them. You copy, review, and send everything yourself.

Data retention and deletion

Your data lives in your browser until you delete it. Removing the extension

removes the data. You can export a JSON backup at any time from Settings.

Licence check

HaulGuard bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, HaulGuard asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with HaulGuard stays

on your computer.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version ships with the extension update and is

posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/haulguard/.
