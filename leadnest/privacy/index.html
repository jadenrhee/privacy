LeadNest Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

LeadNest is built local-first. The short version: the leads you capture are

yours, they are read only from sites you explicitly enabled, and they never

leave your computer.

What LeadNest collects

Nothing on our side. LeadNest runs no server of its own, no analytics, no telemetry, no

error reporting, and no account system of its own. We cannot see your leads or your

browsing, because nothing is ever transmitted to us.

Where your data lives

  • Captured leads, notes, statuses, and settings are stored in your browser

profile using Chrome extension storage and IndexedDB, on your device only.

Deleting a lead deletes it; there is no copy anywhere else.

  • The optional CSV/JSON export writes a file to your computer at your request.
  • LeadNest works fully offline.

What the extension connects to

  • The listing and marketplace sites you enabled. LeadNest reads lead

details (such as a listing, contact prompt, or inquiry) from pages on

Zillow, Apartments.com, and Facebook using content scripts, so it can

capture a lead while you work. It reads these pages **in your browser, using

your existing session**, and only on those sites. It sends nothing from them

to us or anyone else. You can revoke a site's access anytime in

chrome://extensions.

  • ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), only for payments. Handles upgrades via

Stripe; LeadNest stores only a cached paid/unpaid flag locally and sends it

no lead data. See extensionpay.com/privacy and stripe.com/privacy.

  • An optional uninstall survey page opens if you remove the extension. It

is a static page; the link names the product, never you.

What LeadNest never does

  • No selling, sharing, or transmitting of your leads or browsing to us or third

parties.

  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.
  • No reading of pages other than the enabled lead sites.
  • No remote code.

Licence check

LeadNest bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, LeadNest asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with LeadNest stays

on your computer.

Permissions, in plain language

  • storage, alarms: keep your leads and remind you about follow-ups.
  • sidePanel: show your lead workspace beside the page.
  • notifications: follow-up and new-lead reminders.
  • host access to zillow.com, apartments.com, facebook.com: read lead

details from those pages so you can capture them. Nothing leaves your device.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version ships with the extension update and is

posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/leadnest/privacy.
