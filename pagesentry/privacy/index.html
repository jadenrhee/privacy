PageSentry privacy policy

Effective July 9, 2026. Contact: breezyapps.support@gmail.com

PageSentry is local-first. There is no PageSentry server, no account, and no

analytics.

What the extension stores

Your watcher list, page snapshots, change history, and settings are stored in

your browser profile only, using Chrome extension storage and IndexedDB on

your device. Nothing about your watchers or the pages you watch leaves your

device. The optional JSON export writes a file to your computer at your

request.

What the extension connects to

  • The pages you chose to watch. Checking a page fetches that page from

your browser, using your existing session, on the schedule you set. These

requests go only to sites you added, after you granted access to each site

when you added it. You can revoke a site's access at any time in

chrome://extensions.

  • ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), only for payments. If you open the

upgrade page or the extension checks license status, ExtensionPay (a payment

service built on Stripe) processes that. PageSentry sends it no browsing

data and nothing about what you watch. See extensionpay.com/privacy and

stripe.com/privacy.

  • An optional uninstall survey page opens if you remove the extension, so

you can tell us why. Visiting it is your choice; it receives nothing but the

visit.

What the extension never does

  • No selling, sharing, or transmitting of browsing data.
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting.
  • No reading of pages other than the ones you added as watchers.
  • No remote code.

Permissions, in plain terms

  • storage, alarms: keep your watchers and run them on a schedule.
  • notifications: tell you when a watched page changes.
  • scripting, activeTab: the click-to-pick region tool on the page you invoke

it on, and reading JavaScript-heavy pages in a background tab.

  • offscreen: parse fetched pages (the service worker cannot) and play the

optional notification sound.

  • Per-site access is optional and requested only when you add a watcher for

that site. PageSentry never asks for all-sites access at install.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version ships with the extension update and

is posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/pagesentry/privacy.
