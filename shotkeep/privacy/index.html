ShotKeep Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

ShotKeep is built local-first. The short version: your screenshots are yours,

and they never leave your computer.

What ShotKeep collects

Nothing. ShotKeep has no server, no analytics, no telemetry, no error

reporting, and no account system. We cannot see your captures, your browsing,

or anything else, because nothing is ever transmitted to us.

Where your data lives

  • Captures and history are stored in your browser profile using IndexedDB,

on your device only. Deleting a capture deletes it; there is no copy

anywhere else.

  • Settings (format, filename template, size cap) are stored with

chrome.storage.local, also on your device.

  • ShotKeep works fully offline.

The one exception: payments

If you choose to buy Pro, checkout is handled by

ExtensionPay and Stripe on the extensionpay.com

page. ShotKeep stores only a cached paid/unpaid flag locally. We never see or

store your card details, name, or email.

So that a purchase is recognised, ShotKeep asks ExtensionPay for your licence

status when your browser starts and when you open the editor or settings. That

request carries a random installation identifier only — never a capture, a page,

a URL, or anything you have done in the extension. Your screenshots and history

never leave your computer.

Licence check

ShotKeep bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, ShotKeep asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with ShotKeep stays

on your computer.

Permissions, in plain language

  • activeTab: capture the tab you are looking at, only when you click.
  • scripting: show the region-selection crosshair and run the full-page

scroller on that tab, only when you click.

  • downloads: save your finished image to disk.
  • storage: remember your settings.

ShotKeep has no host permissions. It cannot read pages in the background.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version ships with the extension update

and is posted at https://breezylifeapps.com/shotkeep/. The local-only design is

the product; we do not intend to change it.

If you uninstall, Chrome opens a one-question exit survey page

(breezylifeapps.com/uninstall.html?app=shotkeep). It is a static page; the

link names the product, never you.
