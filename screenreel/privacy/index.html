ScreenReel Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

ScreenReel is built local-first. The short version: your recordings are

yours, and they never leave your computer.

What ScreenReel collects

Nothing. ScreenReel runs no server of its own, no analytics, no telemetry, no error

reporting, and no account system of its own. We cannot see your recordings, your screen,

or anything else, because nothing is ever transmitted to us.

Where your data lives

  • Recordings are stored in your browser profile using IndexedDB, on your

device only. Deleting a recording deletes it; there is no copy anywhere

else.

  • Settings (countdown, default source, filename template, library cap)

are stored with chrome.storage.local, also on your device.

  • ScreenReel works fully offline.

What gets recorded

Only what you approve in Chrome's own share dialog, which appears before

every recording. ScreenReel has no host permissions and cannot read the pages

you visit. Closing the share, or clicking Chrome's "Stop sharing", ends the

capture immediately.

The one exception: payments

If you choose to buy Pro, checkout is handled by

ExtensionPay and Stripe on the extensionpay.com

page. ScreenReel stores only a cached paid/unpaid flag locally. We never see

or store your card details, name, or email.

So that a purchase is recognised, ScreenReel asks ExtensionPay for your licence

status when your browser starts and when you open the extension's pages. That

request carries a random installation identifier only — never a recording, a

page, or a URL. Your recordings never leave your computer.

Licence check

ScreenReel bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, ScreenReel asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with ScreenReel stays

on your computer.

Permissions, in plain language

  • storage: remember your settings.
  • downloads: save your finished video to disk when you click Save.

That is the whole list.
