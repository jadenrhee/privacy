ReturnRadar privacy policy

Last updated: 2026-07-09

ReturnRadar holds your purchase history and receipt photos, so the privacy

model is blunt: your data stays on your computer.

What ReturnRadar stores

Items you add (name, store, price, purchase date, return window, warranty),

receipt photos (as compressed images), warranty claim notes, household

labels, and your settings. All of it is stored locally in

chrome.storage.local on your machine.

What ReturnRadar transmits

Nothing, with one narrow exception. The extension has no server, no

analytics, no tracking, no error reporting, and no content scripts. It cannot

read any website and it does not watch you shop. The only network connection

it can make is to ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com), the payment processor, and

that connection carries only payment status for this extension. Payment card

details are handled by Stripe through ExtensionPay and never touch the

extension. Your items, receipts, and photos are never transmitted anywhere.

Chrome sync

ReturnRadar uses chrome.storage.local, which does not sync between

machines. Your records exist only where you created them (use the JSON

backup in Options to move them).

Data deletion

Uninstalling the extension deletes all locally stored data. You can also

export a backup first from Options.

Contact

breezyapps.support@gmail.com

Licence check

ReturnRadar bundles ExtensionPay, which handles the

optional paid upgrade. So a purchase is recognised on this device, ReturnRadar asks

ExtensionPay for your licence status automatically when your browser starts, and

again when you open the extension's own pages — this happens whether or not you

have ever paid. That request carries a random per-installation identifier and

nothing else: no page content, no URLs, no file names, and nothing you have

created in the extension. If you buy Pro, ExtensionPay and Stripe handle the

payment on their own page and your email is held in your ExtensionPay account;

we never see or store your card details. Everything you make with ReturnRadar stays

on your computer.
