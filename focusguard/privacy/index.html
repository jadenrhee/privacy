FocusGuard privacy policy

FocusGuard collects nothing: your block lists, schedules, sessions, and stats

are stored only in your browser's local storage and never leave your device.

That is the whole policy. Some detail for the curious:

  • There is no server, no account, no sign-up, and no analytics of any kind.
  • The extension makes no network requests of its own. The single exception is

the optional Pro upgrade, which is processed by ExtensionPay

(https://extensionpay.com) and Stripe on their pages; FocusGuard stores only

a local paid/unpaid flag and never sees your payment details.

  • If you uninstall, Chrome opens a one-question exit survey page

(breezylifeapps.com/uninstall.html?app=focusguard). It is a static page; the

link names the product, never you.

  • Uninstalling the extension deletes all of its data.
